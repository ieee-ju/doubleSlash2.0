About The Hackathon

Are you looking for a challenging and rewarding experience that will test your skills and creativity to the limits? Join us at Double Slash , an intense six-day journey which will take you on a wild ride of innovation and imagination with solid programming groundwork.The journey begins with brainstorming ideation , ingenuity and innovation presenting the best solution and ends with a high tension climax of development and final pitching. Don't miss out on this chance to push yourself beyond your limits and get set on one of the six tracks to create something truly amazing.Follow the motto ; Code, Commit, and Create !

Registration Process

Double Slash 2.0

The registration would be simply done by just clicking on Register here. Registrations end on 15th January, 2023. Hurry up!

Jan 17 - Jan 19 Brain Storming

Brain Storming Ideas

Double Slash 2.0

Jan 17 - Tracks and Problem Statements to be released at 12 p.m. Participants will start brainstorming for solutions to tackle these real-world problem statements.

Jan 18 - A Discord Meeting will be arranged with all the teams to check on the progress of the solutions they are coming up with and whether they are facing any issues.

Jan 19 - At 12 p.m. sharp, Participants will need to submit their solution in the form of a well-organized presentation (mandatory). In addition to their ppts, participants may also submit Figma/Adobe designs/wireframes relevant to their ideas.

Jan 20 - Result Declaration

Selection of Teams

Double Slash 2.0

At 12 p.m. sharp, we will release the teams with the best ideas will advance to the final offline round of the hackathon.

Jan 21 Development

Double Slash 2.0 Begins

Double Slash 2.0

At 10 a.m., the inaugural ceremony of the final offline round will commence and problem statements for the final round will be provided.

At 12 p.m., finalists will start developing/coding a viable product relevant to the final round problem statements.

Jan 22
Presentation

Judging and Presentation of projects begins

Double Slash 2.0

At 12 p.m., the development round will end.

From 12 p.m., teams will be provided additional 2hrs to prepare a presentation for the product they developed.

From 2 p.m. to 4 p.m., teams will pitch their products in front of esteemed judges.

From 4 p.m. to 5 p.m., winners will be declared, prior to which the award ceremony and the closing ceremony will take place.

FAQs

Answers to the most frequently asked questions!

How many days will the event be organized for?

3 days online prelims and (2+1) day offline finals for selected teams. So if you're in it to win it, consider it to be a 6 day event.

01

What are the registration fees to participate in the event?

Like every good thing in life, it's FREE

02

How many members are needed in each team?

The ideal team size should be 2-3. However, should you choose to play solo, there's no one stopping you. 03

What are Eligibility criteria for participating?

You should be a college student enrolled in a recognized institute and you should have your wits about you, that's it.

04

What is the venue for the event?

In the preliminary online stages, you'll mainly be left to your own accord with a few Discord meets in between. In the later offline stages should you be selected as a finalist, you'll be hosted in KC Roy Hall, Dept. Of Electrical Engineering at JU.

05
